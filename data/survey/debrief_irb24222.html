DEBRIEFING STATEMENT

Thank you for participating in this research study. This form describes the background and purpose of the study in more detail. This is an ongoing project so please do not share this information with others who may participate in the future.

Although the consent form of this study explained that the purpose was to collect data for evaluation, the primary purpose of this study was to evaluate how well a robot can learn language under different conditions.

● STUDY RELATED INFORMATION

The data we collected aids in evaluating the following:

  1. If one version of our language learning system works better than the other.

The above information was not included as part of the consent form or the instruction form because it would have influenced your view of the interaction and the data we got from you.
