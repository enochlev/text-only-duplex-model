Instructions

You will now have two spoken conversations — one with each of two systems (System 1 and System 2), one at a time. After each conversation you will fill out a short questionnaire about that system.

How it works

  • Press ▶ Start. Your browser may ask for microphone permission — click Allow. The first connection can take ~10 seconds while the system warms up.
  • Once the status shows Connected, just talk — the system hears you through the microphone and talks back through the speakers.

What to talk about

Talk naturally to the robot and ask about a variety of topics. Try to include a mix of:

  • Short questions — for example a bit of math, a science fact, or an everyday question ("what should I make for dinner?").
  • Longer, more complex questions — for example "what's the difference between X and Y, and how do they relate?", or any topic you'd like to get into in more depth.

Feel free to interrupt and clarify your question while the bot is still speaking — talking over it is completely fine.

Changing topic

When you want to change topics you can either just keep the current conversation going, or start over fresh with the 🔄 Start new conversation button.

Timing

Watch the timer below the volume meters. Aim for about 3–5 minutes of conversation, but feel free to end early whenever you like by pressing ■ Stop. The conversation ends automatically at 5 minutes.

When you finish, you'll be given a Participant ID and a link to a short questionnaire about that system.
