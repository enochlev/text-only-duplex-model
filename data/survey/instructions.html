Instructions

You will now have two spoken conversations — one with each of two systems (System 1 and System 2), one at a time. After each conversation you will fill out a short questionnaire about that system.

How it works

  • Press ▶ Start. Your browser may ask for microphone permission — click Allow. The first connection can take ~10 seconds while the system warms up.
  • Once the status shows Connected, just talk — the system hears you through the microphone and talks back through the speakers.

What to talk about

Talk naturally and mix it up. Try questions from each group below — use these or make up your own:

Short questions

  • "What's 12 times 8?"
  • "Why is the sky blue?"
  • "How far away is the Moon?"
  • "What should I make for dinner tonight?"
  • "How cold does it get in the desert at night?"

Long questions

  • "What's the difference between a virus and bacteria, and which one do antibiotics work on?"
  • "What's the difference between weather and climate, and how are they related?"
  • "I'm deciding between renting and buying a home — what should I think about?"
  • "How are alligators and crocodiles different, and where does each one live?"
  • "What's the difference between a marathon and a triathlon, and which is harder to train for?"

Questions that need a long answer

  • "Tell me a story about a robot learning to cook."
  • "Walk me through all the planets of the solar system."
  • "Explain how airplanes stay in the air, like I'm ten years old."
  • "Give me step-by-step instructions for making french toast."
  • "Describe what a normal day would look like living on Mars."

Interrupt the robot — while it's mid-answer, jump in with things like:

  • "Wait — can you say that part again?"
  • "Actually, never mind — tell me about something else."
  • "Can you make that shorter?"
  • "Hold on, what does that word mean?"
  • "Okay, I got it — next question."

Talking over the robot is completely fine — that's part of what we're studying.

Changing topic

When you want to change topics you can either just keep the current conversation going, or start over fresh with the 🔄 Start new conversation button.

Timing

Watch the timer below the volume meters. Aim for about 3–5 minutes of conversation, but feel free to end early whenever you like by pressing ■ Stop. The conversation ends automatically at 5 minutes.

When you finish, you'll be given a link to a short questionnaire about that system.
