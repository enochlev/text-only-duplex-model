Instructions

You will now have two spoken conversations — one with each of two systems (System 1 and System 2), one at a time. After each conversation you will fill out a short questionnaire about that system.

How it works

  • Press ▶ Start. Your browser may ask for microphone permission — click Allow. The first connection can take ~10 seconds while the system warms up.
  • Once the status shows Connected, just talk — the system hears you through the microphone and talks back through the speakers.

What to talk about

  • Talk naturally, like you would with a person: say hi, ask it questions, tell it about your day, ask for advice or a story — anything you like. Change topics whenever you want.
  • There is no right or wrong way to have the conversation, and no task to complete.
  • The system can speak while you are speaking, and you can talk over it too — feel free to interrupt it, cut it off, or jump in whenever it feels natural.

When you're done

  • Keep the conversation going for the amount of time the researcher indicates (typically a few minutes), then press ■ Stop.
  • You will then be given a Participant ID number and a link to the questionnaire for that system.

The researcher is nearby the whole time — if anything is unclear or not working, just ask.
